Notes
  • Search bar: I have included a search bar in my website as we have learned that it is a very important part of a well designed website. We have also learned that more than 50% of all users would use a search bar as soon as they open a wesbite rather than looking through the site. My search bar is non-functional, but I have included it to highlight how crucial a search bar is.
  • Responsive: My website is responsive/mobile friendly. I find this massively important due to the fact that a lot of people now use mobile to browse the internet over a laptop or computer. Having a poorly looking website on mobile would turn users away straight away.
  • Color scheme: I have decided to use grayscale as the colouring for my website. I feel this makes the design of the website professional and easy to view, as there are no really bright colours that would turn a user away. In other words, I did not want too much colour saturation. I have made the nav-bar and footer stand out as these are important features in terms of navigation.
  • Contact: I used a non-fucntioning contact form in my website as it is also a good practice of website design. Having a contact form is important as it is a good form of user experience for them to email straight from the website rather than ahving to open a new tab of their email.
  • Gestalt design: I have tried my best to follow Gestalt's Psychology for design by grouping items that are closely related. I used whitespace rather than borders and line for a more simplistic view. All text is aligned and ordered nicely using the same font. Where there is a white background, there is white text. Where there is grey background, there is white text.
